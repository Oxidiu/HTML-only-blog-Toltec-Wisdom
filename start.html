States of Non-Ordinary Reality and Toltec Wisdom

What are those?

What are those you ask? Well, let me tell you. A state of non-ordinary reality is when you alter or enchance your senses and percieve the reality in an unusual way, which it is still reality, but seen from a different perspective. Something like how radio receives different information based on the listening frequency. In the same way, the human can percieve different aspects of the surrounding reality based on his senses modulations and finetuing.

Reality through another eyes

Imagine you try to look at the world trough the eyes of another animal, for example a crow. You would see a completely different reality, for example, a crow is able to see the movement in things that are alive as a light emmanating from them. This way, a crow can tell if something is fully alive or is about to die, because the movement in the later ones has slowed down, and they emmanate less glow.

Also, a dead body which started to rott, is a pleasant sight for a crow because, even though its light has stopped, le little detritivor beings eating the flesh and moving through it, are emanating a different kind of light, each individually. That's why crows stare sometimes hours at a corpse.

How to get there?

1. Drugs

This kind of non-ordinary reality can be induced in many different ways. The most common and widely used way is by consuming different halucinogenic substances, but in a ritualic like manner. For you to achieve an actual sight of reality, there is some knowledge needed, you can not just do drugs like a junkie and expect to get universal wisdom.

For example, there is a cactus called Mescalito or Peyote. It has mescaline, a neuroactive chemical which, of course, if changes the chemistry in the brain, also changes the perception, the interpretation of the reality, it fine tunes your sense and you receive another signal, just like in the example with the radio.

3. Dreaming

Another common way to achieve this kind of states is dreaming. In this way you could potentially travel trough other dimmensions, because you can detach your spirit and percieve the reality through the eys of the spirit whigh has nothing to do with the light.

4. From a Toltec Man of Knowledge

Another less common way to achieve this is to receive the wisdom directly from a toltec sorcerer. Yes, you read right, they still exist, but are hard to find. They are normal people like us, but has the toltec wisdom.
